overview of the movie

The film “Night Market” is a Nigerian fantasy-drama/thriller that explores the idea of a mysterious marketplace where people trade intangible things such as life, fate, and destiny rather than money. The movie blends supernatural elements with emotional human struggles, creating a story that reflects themes of sacrifice, desperation, and the consequences of bargains made in moments of despair. The narrative centers on a strange night market that appears rarely, drawing individuals who are desperate for solutions to their problems. At this market, visitors can make supernatural trades that may grant their deepest wishes—but often at a terrible cost. The film gained attention partly because it marked the acting and producing debut of Nigerian social media influencer Papaya Ex, who used the project to transition into Nollywood filmmaking.

Director

The director is responsible for translating the script into a visual story. In “Night Market,” Ozigi Martin used atmospheric nighttime settings, dramatic lighting, and symbolic scenes to create the eerie and mystical feeling of the market.

Producers

Abike Raheem (popularly known as Papaya Ex) Papaya Ex not only starred in the film but also produced it, making the project a major milestone in her career transition from influencer to filmmaker. As producer, her responsibilities included: Financing and overseeing the film production Assembling the cast and crew Coordinating the distribution and promotion of the movie Her involvement helped attract attention from both social media audiences and Nollywood viewers.
